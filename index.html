Discover the wildlife!

Taking our Diving Master class will change the way you perceive the sea. Providing shelter to approximately 25% of all marine life on the planet, reefs are not only home to millions of species, but are also a fundamental part of the balance of ecosystems both at sea and on land.

Throughout the Riviera Maya we can find 65 species of coral and more than 500 species of mollusks, fish and crustaceans. The arrival of the whale shark, bull shark, sailfish and the spawning season of 4 of the 7 species of sea turtles are examples of beautiful events that, in addition to being fundamental to the ecosystem, are also of great attraction for lovers of the nature .
